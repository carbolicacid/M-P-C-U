4. Take a multi-digit number as an input using serial monitor. After that take 4 single-digit
numbers from keypad. Consider you have two (2) LEDs (LED1 & LED0) attached to your
Arduino along with your keypad. Now LEDs would indicate how many single digit
numbers are factors of multi-digit number. If all of these 4 numbers are factors then both
LED would blink.

Example
Multi-Digit Input: 35 (From Serial Monitor)
Single Digit Inputs: 2 5 7 9 (Using Keypad)
LED1 → ON LED0 → OFF (0b10 = 2) [As 5 & 7 are factors of 35]
Multi-Digit Input: 5 (From Serial Monitor)
Single Digit Inputs: 2 3 4 5 (Using Keypad)
LED1 → OFF LED0 → ON (0b01 = 1) [As only 5 is factor of 5]
Multi-Digit Input: 20 (From Serial Monitor)
Single Digit Inputs: 1 2 4 5 (Using Keypad)
Both LED1 & LED0 would blink [As all four numbers are factors of 20]
